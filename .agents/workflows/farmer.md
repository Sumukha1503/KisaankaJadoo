# Farmer Workflow

As a Farmer on KisaanKaJadoo, your primary goal is to manage your farm efficiently using AI and connect with markets/labour.

### 1. Farm Planning & AI Guidance
- Navigate to **Farm Wizard** to get seasonal crop recommendations.
- Use the **AI Assistant** (Voice/Text) to ask about weather, soil, or pest control.

### 2. Disease Scanning
- Go to the **AI Scanner** page.
- Upload or take a photo of a diseased leaf.
- Receive instant diagnosis and treatment suggestions.

### 3. Labour Management
- **Post a Task**: Navigate to **Labour Hub** or **My Business** to post a new task (e.g., harvesting).
- **Match & Book**: Use the matching algorithm to find nearby labourers and book them.
- **Attendance**: Use the QR code generated in your booking to verify labourer attendance.

### 4. Market Trading
- **List Crops**: Go to **Vendors** and create a listing for your harvest.
- **Negotiate**: Receive and accept/reject bids from vendors in your **My Business** dashboard.

### 5. Shopping
- Browse the **Shop** for seeds, fertilizers, and tools.
- Place orders and track them in your profile.
