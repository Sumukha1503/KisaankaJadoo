# Vendor & Store Owner Workflow

Manage your inventory and trade with farmers.

### 1. Inventory Management (Store Owner)
- Use the **My Business** dashboard to add new products (Seeds, Fertilizers, Tools).
- Monitor stock levels and update prices as needed.

### 2. Market Bidding (Vendor)
- Browse the **Vendor Market** to see crops listed by farmers.
- **Place Bids**: Submit price offers for bulk purchases.
- **Track Offers**: Monitor your "Pending" and "Negotiating" offers in the **My Business** dashboard.

### 3. Order Processing
- View incoming orders from farmers in the **Management** section.
- Updates status from 'Pending' to 'Shipped' to 'Delivered'.
