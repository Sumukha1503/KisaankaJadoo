# Admin Workflow

Monitor and oversee the entire KisaanKaJadoo platform.

### 1. Platform Analytics
- The **Analytics** page serves as your command center.
- Monitor Total Users, Revenue, Active Listings, and AI Scan success rates.

### 2. User Oversight
- View the **User Role Breakdown** to see community growth across farmers, labourers, and vendors.
- Monitor recently active tasks and market transactions.

### 3. Management
- Admins have access to all modules to ensure platform integrity.
- Use the global stats to identify high-demand areas (e.g., specific districts needing more vehicles).
