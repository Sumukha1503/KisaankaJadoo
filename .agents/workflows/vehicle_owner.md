# Vehicle Owner Workflow

Rent out your agricultural machinery to farmers.

### 1. Listing Vehicles
- Navigate to **My Business** and click **Add New Vehicle**.
- Provide details: Model (e.g., Mahindra Tractor), Rate per Hour, and Location.

### 2. Managing Rentals
- Your vehicles will appear in the **Vehicle Rental** marketplace for farmers.
- View active and upcoming bookings in your management dashboard.

### 3. Availability
- Toggle vehicle availability (Live/Offline) to control when your fleet is visible to farmers.
