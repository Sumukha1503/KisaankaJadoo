# Labour Workflow

As a Labourer on KisaanKaJadoo, you can find agricultural work and manage your assignments.

### 1. Finding Work
- Your profile is automatically listed in the **Labour Market** based on your skills (set during registration or in Profile).
- Farmers will find you via the matching algorithm based on your location and expertise.

### 2. Managing Assignments
- Check the **Labour Hub** for available tasks in your area.
- Once booked, you will see the task details in your **My Business** dashboard.

### 3. Verification & Payment
- When you reach the farm, show your **Profile QR** or the booking details to the Farmer for attendance.
- Payments are handled according to the agreement (escrow/deposit shown in booking).
